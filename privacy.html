Privacy Policy

Last updated: 2026

Mild ("we", "our", or "the app") respects your privacy and is committed to protecting your information. This Privacy Policy explains how information is collected, used, and protected when you use the Mild mobile application, website, and related online services provided by us.

Information We Collect

Mild may collect limited information required to provide the functionality of the application, including:

  • Ingredients entered by text, voice, or photo
  • Usage data related to how the application is used
  • Device information such as device type, operating system, and app version
  • Basic analytics data used to improve the application

Mild does not collect sensitive personal information such as contacts, personal files, or messages.

AI Processing

Ingredient recognition from photos and voice input may be processed using artificial intelligence services provided by OpenAI. Photos and voice inputs are used only to identify ingredients and generate recipe results within the application. These inputs are processed to analyze items and convert them into searchable ingredient data used to provide relevant recipe suggestions.

How We Use Information

Information collected may be used to:

  • Provide recipe search and ingredient recognition features
  • Generate recipe suggestions based on ingredients
  • Improve application performance and user experience
  • Analyze usage patterns to enhance the functionality of the app
  • Maintain security and fix technical issues

Data Retention

Information processed by the app is stored only as long as needed to provide and improve the service. We do not create or store personal user profiles and do not retain personal data long term. Technical or usage information is used only to operate and improve the service and may be removed when no longer required.

Data Security

We take reasonable technical and organizational measures to protect information from unauthorized access, misuse, alteration, or disclosure. These measures include secure infrastructure, controlled access to data, and industry-standard security practices designed to help safeguard the information processed by the application.

Third-Party Services

The application may use third-party services for analytics, cloud infrastructure, or AI processing. These services may process limited technical information required to operate and improve the functionality of the app. Any information handled by third-party providers is processed according to their own privacy policies and security practices.

User Rights

Users may request information about the data processed by the application or request deletion of their data by contacting us. We will review and respond to such requests in accordance with applicable data protection regulations and within a reasonable period of time as required by law.

Children's Privacy

Mild is not intended for children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children. If we become aware that such information has been submitted through the application, we will take immediate steps to remove it from our records and systems as quickly as possible.

Website Cookies

Our website may use basic cookies to improve performance and understand how visitors use the site. These cookies help analyze traffic, improve functionality, remember basic preferences, and ensure the website operates properly, securely, and efficiently for all users and visitors.

Changes to This Privacy Policy

This Privacy Policy may be updated from time to time. Any updates will be posted on this page with the revised date. We encourage users to review this page periodically to stay informed about how information is protected and how the application handles user data.
